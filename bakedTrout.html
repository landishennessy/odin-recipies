Baked Trout

Description

Baked trout is a classic and easy way to cook one of the worlds favorite freshwater fish!

This recipie is usually coupled with s trout fresh from the river and can be done right on the banks of s river in a firepit as well.

Ingrediants

  • A fresh trout
  • aluminum foil
  • salt and pepper
  • lemon

Steps

  1. gut and clean fish
  2. fill fish with goodies
  3. cook for 10-15 min
  4. serve over rice
