Cheesy Ramen

Description

Cheezy ramen is a delicacy thst can be surpassed only by the finest hood quisine. If youre feeling free in the kitchen and want to give a shout out to the homies still strugglin, this dish is for you!

This recipie uses whatever pre-packaged noodles you may already hsve on hand and is not picky. As for the cheese, that must be some form of american cheese. That part is not up for discussion. For the soup, i like spicy seafood.

Ingrediants

  • Ramen Noodles
  • American Cheese
  • Tenacity

Steps

  1. make ramen noodles according to package
  2. Add cheese to the ramen noodles
  3. Stir
